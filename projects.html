if you are a human in this world, I bet you heard of the environmental impacts caused by meat consumption. But do you know where they happen? And how can we link the beef that we had for dinner yesterday to the real impacts happening elsewehere during production?


We traced some of these impacts by modeling animal and meat movements around the United States. In the circular chart, you can see "virtual water" flows between States and find where and how much water was used to produce beef that was consumed during a year in a given state.
